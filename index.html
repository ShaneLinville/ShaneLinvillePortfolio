Hello, I am Shane.

My career over the last 16 years has gone from working in a distribution center as an order filler to working in IT for Hallmark Cards. I have always believed art is created for users to enjoy and consume and to be filled with joy. When I began to learn Front End development I began to see the possibilities to create something meaningful when coding.
